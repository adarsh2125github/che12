Get Involved in Biodiversity Conservation

There are several ways you can get involved in biodiversity conservation:

Increase your knowledge and awareness: Understand environmental issues and the impacts of biodiversity loss1.

Support local farms: Regularly buying from small local farmers at stands or markets helps to keep dollars in the local economy and supports agricultural efforts to conserve biodiversity2.

Save the bees: Plant nectar-producing wildflowers in your backyard, or even build bee boxes for local bees to call home2.

Plant local flowers, fruits, and vegetables: Research the flora, fruits, and veggies native to your area, and plant a variety in your backyard or a hanging garden2.

Conserve water: Biodiversity depends on the abundance of local fresh water. Taking five-minute showers and turning the water off while washing your hands, doing the dishes, or brushing your teeth are all easy ways to conserve water2.

Respect local habitats: Protect local biodiversity by sticking to the walking path or hiking trail2.

Know the source: Check the products you buy and the companies you support to ensure that your buying habits are not contributing to the destruction of habitat elsewhere2.

Support government policies and actions: Increase support for government policies and actions that conserve our valuable ecosystems1.

Get involved in various fields: Conservation is not just about biology or science, it's also about politics, economics, awareness, education, law enforcement, socioecology, social justice, etc3.
